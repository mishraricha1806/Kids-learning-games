BrightSteps Privacy Policy

BrightSteps is a kids learning app designed to work locally on the device.

  • We do not collect personal information from children.
  • We do not require login.
  • We do not collect location.
  • We do not use camera, contacts, or personal identifiers.
  • Microphone is used only inside Talking Buddy when allowed, and recordings stay on the device during play.
  • Progress is stored locally on the device.
  • No third-party analytics or third-party advertising are enabled in this version.

Parents

Parents can reset local progress from the parent dashboard inside the app.
